Introduction

We aimed to develop an end-to-end working platform integrated with real time conversational abilities that generates curiosity and provides an interactive experience-based learning to the students intending to learn about the lives, contributions and achievements of famous personalities.

Workflow

a) Employed a generalized model capable of processing images of the personality using online scrapping and Deepfake (Transfer Learning). b) Developed a Deep Learning model over it that generates an informative summarized text about the personality. c) Applied NLP to it to create a question-answer based model trained specifically to answer the relevant live questions asked by the end-user.

Final Thoughts / Future Scope

We can make a specialized model, we can also make digital humans like you and me and upload them to our database.
